About

I’m Hudson Justice, 21, from Arizona. I’ve always been drawn to the way light bends across a room, the way a fleeting gesture can hold a universe of feeling. I chase those moments with a camera in hand, not just to capture them, but to let them breathe, linger, and speak to anyone who sees them.

Studio Justice isn’t just a name, it’s my middle name, my compass, the lens through which I see the world. Every project I touch is a chance to honor the subtle, the overlooked, the quietly powerful. I believe in intention: every frame, every cut, every shadow and highlight is a choice, and those choices are how stories take shape and hearts move.

My work is colored by my love for vintage Italian elegance, the careful artistry of designer brands, and the way old and new can collide to make something timeless. I’m drawn to composition that feels effortless but is carefully considered, to movement that whispers rather than shouts, to moments that hold both nostalgia and possibility.

I collaborate with musicians, storytellers, and visionaries who refuse the ordinary, who see beauty in detail, emotion in nuance. From the first spark of an idea to the final frame, I obsess over light, movement, and feeling, aiming for work that lingers, resonates, and reminds people that imagery can be as alive as memory itself.

At the end of the day, my work is me. My curiosity, my heart, my perspective. It’s an invitation: to pause, to feel, to remember that the world is made of moments worth holding.

Hudson Lee · Director · Cinematographer
